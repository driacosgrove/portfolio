Fashion Thrift Flips

Ever since I got into fashion, I fell in love with thrifting. I loved the idea of finding cool and unique pieces that not everyone was buying. Soon after this, I started creating visions in my head of pieces I wanted to own, but could not find in retail nor while thrifting. This is when I discovered my potential in thrift flipping, where I buy garments second hand for the fabric, design, embellishments, or another aspect of the clothing, and then turn them into a different piece. Upcycling is a super sustainable fashion option where you not only buy second hand and support thrift stores, but also are able to use your own creativity and potential into creating one of a kind pieces.

  • Exhibit 1: Transforms a modern day blouse into a unique skirt and top set.
  • Exhibit 2: Takes a bland crochet long sleeve and elevates it to a more modern, accessorized tank top.
    Exhibit 3: Takes a new look on a conservative denim midi skirt and transforms it into a trendy, young, two piece fashion look.
    Exhibit 4: Brings an everyday basic into a one of a kind shirt.
    Exhibit 5: Takes an every day basic tank and adds metal chains and embellishments, while changing the neckline into a much more interesting piece.
